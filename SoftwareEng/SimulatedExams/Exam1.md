### Section D: Case Study and Scenario-Based Modeling (15 Marks)
*Read the scenario below and answer the tasks that follow.*

**Scenario:**  
A regional retail corporation, "QuickBuy," is planning to upgrade its internal inventory and supplier management system. Currently, they use an old legacy system that does not support modern web services. The management wants to replace it by purchasing a Commercial Off-The-Shelf (COTS) inventory system and configuring it to integrate with their existing web-based point-of-sale systems. They have identified three candidate systems that could be customized to fit their needs. The project manager wants to ensure they minimize the risk of building everything from scratch.

#### Tasks:
1. **Model Recommendation & Justification (5 Marks):** Identify the software process model most appropriate for QuickBuy's project. Justify your choice with two (2) reasons based on the scenario.
2. **Process Stages (5 Marks):** List and describe the five (5) stages of the process model you recommended, detailing what actions QuickBuy will take in each stage.
3. **Evaluation (5 Marks):** State one (1) major advantage and one (1) major disadvantage of using this model for QuickBuy's system upgrade.
